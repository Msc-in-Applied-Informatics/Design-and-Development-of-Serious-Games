PURPOSE OF PROJECT:  
YONKO GAME is a 2D strategy and adventure game where the player must collect various items and place them inside a house to charge a magical Totem and win the game. The player must face environmental dangers like poisonous snakes, manage limited health ("Water"), and collect bonus items such as eggs and food. The game promotes strategic thinking, resource management, and quick decision-making.
